Frequently Asked Questions
What phone model do I need?
Our app supports both iOS and Android. We recommend using a smartphone with an ultra-wide-angle lens to effectively showcase the size of the rooms.
Do I need to use a tripod?
Using a tripod can provide a more stable shot and facilitate HDR synthesis in post-production. However, you can also shoot handheld. In well-lit conditions, we only need you to maintain maximum stability for 5 seconds.
How can I shoot better with recam?
“Squat, Settle, Stabilize”. The abdominal framing height is most suitable for shooting real estate photos. Maintain framing posture both horizontal and vertical for minimal cropping. During the shoot, aim to stay motionless for 5 seconds to prevent unwanted blurring.
Can I modify or ask for more advanced services?
